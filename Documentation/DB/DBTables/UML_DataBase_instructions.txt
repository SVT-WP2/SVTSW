These are the instructions to use the online tool https://dbdiagram.io/ in order to create UML diagrams of the DataBase

1) Go to https://dbdiagram.io and click in the "button" Create your diagram. 

2) You will be redirect it to a screen where in the left side of the webpage there is a text editor

3) There you always have an example that you can delete and paste your code(see the tables.txt in this folder)

4) Make sure you get a free acount so you can export your DataBase UML as a PDF that can be included in presenations
